Pulau Sipadan, Sabah

Explore the rich ecological and cultural heritage of Pulau Sipadan, Sabah, through our dedicated digital archive. The Pulau Sipadan Web Portal Repository is designed to preserve and make accessible a wealth of information that highlights the island's unique biodiversity and the traditions of its indigenous communities. This comprehensive resource supports researchers, educators, conservationists, and the general public by providing a robust collection of multimedia materials, documents, and records.

Our commitment to ethical management and preservation of digital content is upheld by strict adherence to Malaysia’s Intellectual Property Corporation Act 2002 and the National Archive Act 2003. With advanced web archiving technologies and rigorous quality assurance processes, we ensure that every piece of content remains accurate, reliable, and available for future generations.

Join us in exploring and preserving the wonders of Pulau Sipadan.
